Projection Changelog

Tracking how picks evolve from projected to confirmed lineups

Every night at 9 PM ET, the model runs with projected lineups to generate preview picks. As confirmed lineups are released the next day, the model re-runs and picks may shift. This page tracks those changes — over time it reveals how reliable the night-before preview is and how much lineup confirmation actually moves the needle.
