Model Analysis

Wheeler is the better pitcher and nobody's pretending otherwise, but a plus-money dog against an ace is a price about how the other eight innings go, and Cleveland's bullpen plus Philly's middle relief is not the gap the board is selling. At +166 you're getting a one-run game priced like a two-run loss, and the late innings are where this ticket cashes.
